# 📘 Product Leadership Playbook

> Practical frameworks, operating models, and leadership practices for building modern Product organizations.

Welcome!

This repository is a collection of the frameworks, templates, and operating models I've developed while leading Product organizations in enterprise software.

Rather than focusing on theory, this playbook captures practical approaches that have helped teams improve alignment, reduce delivery friction, and make better product decisions.

Whether you're leading a Product team, scaling an organization, or simply looking for ideas to improve your own processes, I hope you find something useful here.

# Guiding Principles

Some of the principles that shape this playbook:

- Customers buy outcomes, not features.
- Product exists to reduce uncertainty.
- Engineering owns implementation. Product owns business decisions.
- Discovery is continuous, not a phase.
- Small, incremental delivery beats large releases.
- Measure outcomes over output.
- Strong relationships outperform perfect processes.
- AI should amplify Product thinking, not replace it.
